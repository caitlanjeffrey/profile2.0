PROFESSIONAL SUMMARY

My years as a lead business analyst for a precision agriculture company opened my eyes to the world of development. I fell in love with the process of designing UI/UX for mobile devices, writing requirements for new features, communicating with users, potential clients and business partners on improvements and even the opportunity for a field trip to visit our users in action.

My favorite part of the day was working and communicating with various teams throughout the company to build a product that met the needs of every user who downloaded the application.

Those years laid a foundation for my movement towards the development side. I’ve combined my love of designing user-friendly design with actually building those pages and processes one by one. There is a sense of satisfaction with being behind a working product. Since that first day in class where I opened my first HTML5 page, I haven’t looked back.

I’m hooked.
